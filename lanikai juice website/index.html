Lanikai Juice Website

I redesigned Lanikai Juice website. Lanikai juice is founded in 1997, Lanikai Juice Hawaii is a food and beverage company that focuses on making and serving healthy and fresh smoothies, fruit bowls, freshly squeezed juices, food, and cold pressed juices. Based on the island of O’ahu, Lanikai Juice Hawaii is strategically located in downtown Kailua, Kahala, Haleiwa, Waikiki (Hilton Hawaiian Village), Kapolei and Kaka’ako.

Ideation

Sketching

I designed a website aimed at maximizing the expression of healthy juices with a variety of colors and fruits found in Lanikai Juice. The goal was to create a vibrant site by matching a lively font and colors, capturing a playful vibe.

Wire Frame

Essential Features

Hero Carousol

I focused on simple usage without the need for complex launches. Users can log in once to save their bus information and continue using it without hassle. Emphasizing convenience for quick access at any time over repetitive and time-consuming processes was my priority. Additionally, I aimed to provide users with the most necessary services they might need.

Finalizing

Final Result

I strived to break away from the conventional framework of Lanikai Juice's existing site and create a design that is lively and engaging for viewers. My aim was to craft a bright and dimensional website, exuding a playful and dynamic feel. In the hero section, I aimed to emphasize the characteristics of their juices more strongly, while throughout the website, I endeavored to represent the texture and colors of their juices.
