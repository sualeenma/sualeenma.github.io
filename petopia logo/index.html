Petopia

Petopia is a place that sells pet supplies. It also offers grooming and bathing services for pets. I've created a simple logo for it.

Ideation

Sketching

I wanted to create a logo featuring the most popular pets, cats and dogs. I combined their images with their paw prints to create a simple yet clear symbol that unmistakably represents pets.

Finalizing

Final Result

I selected one of the sketches and completed it as an illustration. Without adding any colors, I combined it with a matching font to finish the logo.
