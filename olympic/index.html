Olympic Pictogram

I've created pictograms representing archery, basketball, weightlifting, and rhythmic gymnastics, selected from the Summer Olympics sports. The goal was to ensure clear and dynamic depictions of the actions.

Ideation

Sketching

Taking clarity of action and dynamic visuals as the primary focus, I experimented with various sketches. Since the goal was to create four pictograms, I sketched different sports and selected the ones with the most distinct style. After gathering them, I refined and finalized the pictograms.

Finalizing

Final Result

I created the basic poster for the Olympics using pictograms. The background of the poster is kept in light, neutral colors to ensure the focus remains on the central elements: the pictograms representing the sports and the Olympic logo.
