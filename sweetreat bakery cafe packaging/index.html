Sweetreat Bakery Cafe

Sweetreat Cafe catches the eyes of customers with its trendy and visually-pleasing interior and packaging design. Its luxurious packaging compliments the many delicious pastries that are well-known by
the public.

Ideation

Sketching

When I think of a bakery caf√©, sweetness comes to mind, and when I think of sweetness, I often think of a bear who loves honey. That is why I wanted to use a bear in the logo. I used an AI tool to draw a bear on a whiteboard. Then, I brought the bear into Illustrator, vectorized it, made some adjustments, and used it in the logo.

Essential Features

pattern

After that, I created patterns for the packaging. I illustrated items like cupcakes, whisks, and coffee beans, and incorporated them into the pattern, ensuring it was diverse yet not overly complex. For the packaging colors, I used only five distinct colors.

Finalizing

Final Result

Using bright and appetizing colors, I created trendy and cute packaging. I designed pastry boxes, menus, stickers, cups, and belly bands. I carefully arranged the patterns to avoid giving a cluttered impression.
