Lanikai Juice Website

I redesigned Lanikai Juice website. Based on the island of O’ahu, Lanikai Juice was founded in 1997, and is a food and beverage company that focuses on making and serving healthy and fresh smoothies, fruit bowls, food, and freshly squeezed and cold pressed juices. It is strategically located in downtown Kailua, Kahala, Haleiwa, Waikiki, Kapolei and Kaka’ako.

Ideation

Sketching

I designed a website aimed at maximizing the expression of healthy juices with a variety of colors and fruits found in Lanikai Juice. The goal was to create a vibrant site by matching lively fonts and colors, capturing a playful vibe.

Essential Features

Hero Carousel

I wanted to showcase the diverse fruits and colorful juices that symbolize Lanikai Juice in the hero section of the website. I used a circular carousel to display them.

Finalizing

Final Result

I strived to break away from the conventional framework of Lanikai Juice's existing site and create a design that is lively and engaging for viewers. My aim was to craft a bright and dimensional website, exuding a playful and dynamic feel. In the hero section, I aimed to emphasize the characteristics of their juices more strongly, while throughout the website, I endeavored to represent the texture and colors of their juices.
